# Professional Fellowship Certificate Masterclass System - User Guide

**Website Purpose:** Apply for prestigious fellowship certifications (FIBAKM, FCBA, FCKM) and manage your application through a complete online system with payment processing and document management.

**Access:** Public registration available. Login required to submit applications.

---

## Powered by Manus

This system is built with cutting-edge technology:

**Frontend:** React 19 + TypeScript + Tailwind CSS 4 + shadcn/ui components for a modern, responsive user interface

**Backend:** Next.js with tRPC for type-safe API procedures, ensuring end-to-end type safety from database to frontend

**Database:** PostgreSQL on Neon with Drizzle ORM for robust data management

**Authentication:** OAuth2-based authentication with role-based access control (user and admin roles)

**Payments:** Paystack integration for secure online payment processing

**File Storage:** Google Drive integration for secure document storage and management

**Deployment:** Auto-scaling infrastructure with global CDN for reliable, fast access worldwide

## Using Your Website

### Step 1: Create Your Account
Click the **"Login"** button on the homepage. You'll be guided through a secure authentication process. Once logged in, you'll see your personalized dashboard.

### Step 2: Start a New Application
From your dashboard, click **"New Application"** to begin. You'll be guided through a multi-step form covering:
- Personal information (name, email, contact details)
- Programme selection (cohort and fellowship tracks)
- Academic and professional qualifications
- Employment history and experience
- Statement of purpose (minimum 250 words)

### Step 3: Upload Supporting Documents
In the application details page, upload required documents:
- Academic certificates (Bachelor's, Master's, PhD)
- Professional certifications (CBA, CKM, ACA, ACCA, etc.)
- Curriculum Vitae (CV)
- Passport photograph
- Identification document (National ID, International Passport, or Driver's License)

All documents are securely stored in Google Drive and linked to your application.

### Step 4: Pay Application Fee
Once your application is complete, proceed to payment. Click **"Pay Now"** and you'll be redirected to Paystack's secure payment gateway. The application fee will be displayed clearly before payment.

### Step 5: Track Your Application
Return to your dashboard anytime to view your application status:
- **Draft:** Application not yet submitted
- **Submitted:** Awaiting admin review
- **Under Review:** Admin is evaluating your application
- **Approved:** Congratulations! You've been accepted
- **Declined:** Application was not approved

### Step 6: Download Your Certificate
Once approved, a **"Certificate"** button will appear on your application. Click to download your official fellowship certificate with your post-nominals (FIBAKM, FCBA, or FCKM).

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I save my application as draft?**
A: Yes! Your application is automatically saved as a draft. You can edit it anytime before submission.

**Q: What happens after I submit my application?**
A: Your application enters the review queue. Admins will evaluate it and notify you of the decision via email.

**Q: Can I upload documents after submitting my application?**
A: Yes, you can continue to upload or modify documents even after submission, as long as your application is under review.

**Q: How long does the review process take?**
A: Typically 5-10 business days. You'll receive email notifications at each stage.

**Q: What if my application is declined?**
A: You can apply again in the next cohort. Contact support for feedback on your previous application.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes. All personal data is encrypted and processed in compliance with NDPA 2023 and GDPR standards.
